About

Stories is meant to better track your Scrum Team's user Stories and help organize you product and sprint backlogs. This website allows you to create new projects and Stories to add to your product backlog, which you can then move to your sprint backlog. You will be able to input the ID, priority, estimate, spike, status, assignment, and description within the site and it will be added to your product backlog automatically. You will then be able to move these Stories to the sprint backlog when your team is ready to do so.
